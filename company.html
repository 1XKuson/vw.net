Our Company

Established Legacy

Visual Web Co., Ltd. was founded on May 1998. It is incorporated and located in Bangkok, Thailand.

Growth & Evolution

We had started out as a provider of web hosting and web site design. Web hosting was initially provided on servers in the USA and in Thailand only. In 2003 we added dedicated servers to our services and, in later years, also co-location services, virtual servers (VPS, VDS, etc.) and cloud servers.

In recent years we have also widened the range of our offered server locations from initially only including Thailand and the USA. During these last years we had been specializing in providing servers in Asian data centers and have included Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Saudi Arabia, a lot of other Asian and also Middle Eastern locations and also Australia and New Zealand in our range of offered server locations. Since 2015 we have been offering servers in more than 100 locations all over the world. New locations and solutions are added constantly.
